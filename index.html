Hearthstone Deck Tracker

The Hearthstone Deck Tracker is a powerful tool for avid Hearthstone players. It offers the following features:

  • Advanced deck tracking: Keep track of the cards in your deck, the cards you have drawn, and the cards your opponent has played.
  • Personalized statistics: Analyze your gameplay with detailed statistics, such as win rate, average mana curve, and card draw percentages.
  • Deck recommendations: Get suggestions for optimizing your deck based on popular meta decks and current win rates.
